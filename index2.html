Hi! I'm Sanjeet Mandal, a passionate Full Stack Developer currently pursuing B.Tech in Computer Science & Engineering at Lovely Professional University, Punjab. I love building things that live on the internet — from smooth, responsive frontends to reliable and scalable backend systems.
